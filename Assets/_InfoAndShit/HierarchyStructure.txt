 - scene specific orchestrated classes, they control the scene
	- e.g. the flow of the game, units, ...

2. Setup:
- lights, camera, sounds, ...

3. Environment
- tiles of a grid
- units
- general objects
- player

4. Canvases
- Menus
- Overlays

5. Testing

6. Systems
- only objects that have "dont destroy on load"
	- e.g. ResourceSystem, DebugSystem, SaveSystem, ...
